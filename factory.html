We have Invested in both Machines & in People

We believe that all of our high-end computerised machines are as good as our operators, and our quality speaks for itself . That is why we have taken all the necessary steps to set up an exemplary industry standard in the glass processing in the South Asian region.

With our current purpose-built brand new production facility and with the current investment plans over the next two years, we want to build one of the biggest glass processing facility in the South Asia and meet the demand of the ever expanding glass market in this rapidly globalising world. Partnering with the global glass technology giant like, Saint-Gobain, Dip-Tech, Intermac, Lisec, Forel, Botero and Northglass we are in the forefront of custom build production line in Bangladesh.

"It is a great place to work where we do indeed feel part of the AEG family. Along with the decent payment skims here at anglo eastern glass, there is an amazing opportunity for skill development."

We are Committed to Work with Exceptional Raw Material

We firmly believe that glass fabrication begins with exceptional raw materials and that is why we work in partnership with the world’s most reliable and high quality suppliers of float glass including St Gobain, Pilkington, PPG, Guardian, AGC & NSG. These float lines have supplied glass to the glass processors around the world for high-profile and groundbreaking international architectural projects.
